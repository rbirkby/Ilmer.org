In the Name of God Amen I John Bowden of Ilmer in the County of Bucks yeo[man]\
being of Sound & disposeing Minde & Memory praised be God But being Mindfull of\
My Mortality doe this p[re]sent that I may not Departe this life without haveing\
first setled & ordered things according to my mind Doe therefore make & ordaine\
this my last Will & Testamt: in Manner following Comitting then my Soule into\
the hands of Almighty God hopeing for Salvation through Jesus Christ my Redeemer\
And appointing my body to be decently buryed at the Discretion of my Executrix\
herein after named as touching the goods & worldly Estate wherewith it hath\
pleased Almighty God to blesse me I dispose of the same as followeth Imprimis I\
give & Devise unto Anne my loveing wife for & dureing the Terme of her naturall\
life All that my Messuage or Tenemt: with the appurten[an]ces wherein I now\
dwell scituate & being in Ilmer aforesd: in the said County of Bucks & all &\
every the houses Edifices Buildings Barnes Stables Outhouses Yards Orchards\
Gardens & Backsides whatsoever to the said Messuage or Tenemt: belonging used or\
in any wise app[er]teyning And alsoe all & every my ffreehold Closes peices &\
p[ar]cells of Arrable Land Meadow pasture & Ley ground whatsoever with their &\
every of their appurten[an]ces lyeing & being within the p[ar]ishes of Ilmer\
aforesd: Saunderton & Kingsey or either of them in the said County of Bucks &\
Conteyning altogether by Estimac[i]on ffifty Acres be the same more or lesse\
Item my Will is And I doe hereby give & Devise unto My Daughter Anne the now\
wife of William Woolven One Annuity or yearly Rent Charge of Tenn pounds of good\
& Lawfull money of Great Brittain without any manner of Defalcac[i]on or\
abatemt: to be made thereof or of any p[ar]te or p[ar]cell thereof for by reason\
of or in Liew of any Taxes Assessmts: or other Charges whatsoever Imposed or to\
be Imposed on the Lands or on the said Rent or Annuity by any Act or Acts of\
Parliamt: or otherwise howsoever the said Annuity or yearly Rent Charge of Tenn\
pounds to be yearly yssueing & goeing out of all & every my said Messuage Closes\
Lands Tenemts: Meadow pasture Ley ground & hereditamts: & p[re]misses herein\
before menconed & to be by her my said Daughter had held received p[er]ceived\
taken & enjoyed from & Imediately after the Decease of her the said Anne my wife\
for & dureing the Naturall life of her the said Anne my Daughter & to be paid\
unto her my said Daughter or her Assignes at foure usuall ffeasts or dayes of\
paymt: in the yeare (that is to say) at the ffeasts of St: Michaell the\
ArchAngell, the Nativity of o[u]r: Saviour Christ The Annunciac[i]on of the\
blessed Virgin Mary & the Nativity of St: John the Baptist by even & equall\
porc[i]ons the ffirst paymt: thereof to be made at such of the said ffeasts as\
shall first & next happen after the decease of her the said Anne my wife Item my\
Will is And I doe hereby further Devise & appoint That if it shall happen the\
said yearly Rent of Tenn pounds or any p[ar]te thereof to be behind or unpaid in\
p[ar]te or in all by the space of Twenty dayes next over or after any of the\
said ffeasts or dayes of paymt: on which as

Aforesd: the same ought to be paid That then & soo often & at all times from\
thenceforth it shall & may be Lawfull to & for the said Anne my Daughter & her\
Assignes into all & every my said Messuage Closes Lands Tenemts: Meadow pasture\
Ley grounds hereditamts: & p[re]misses aforemenconed & into every p[ar]te &\
p[ar]cell thereof to Enter & Distreyne & the Distresse & Distresses soe there &\
from time to time to be had & found to take lead Drive & Carry away & Impound &\
in pound to deteyne & keepe untill the said Annuity or yearly Rent Charge of\
Tenn pounds with the Arrearages thereof if any be shall be fully satisfyed &\
payed ITEM from & after the decease of the said Anne my wife (saveing reserveing\
& preserving the said yearly Rent of Tenn pounds unto & for the said Anne my\
Daughter with the power & meanes of obteyning thereof according to the purport &\
Intent of this my Will) I doe hereby give Will & Devise all & every my said\
Messuage Closes Lands Tenemts: Meadow pasture Ley ground hereditamts: &\
p[re]misses herein before menconed with their & every of their appurten[an]ces\
unto my Loveing ffriends William Woolven of West Grimsted in the County of\
Sussex yeom my son in Law George ffranklyn the Elder of Haddenham in the County\
of Bucks aforesd: yeom & Edward Stone the younger of Princes Risborough in the\
same County yeom their Exec[uto]rs: Adm[inistrato]rs: & Assignes for & dureing\
the Terme of six yeares to be Accompted from the time of the Decease of the said\
Anne my wife fully to be Compleat & ended if John Woolven my Grandson sonne of\
my said Daughter Anne shall soe long live Upon Speciall Trust & Confidence\
neverthelesse in them the said William Woolven George ffranklyn & Edward Stone &\
the Survivour & Survivours of them & the Executours & Adm[inistrato]rs: of the\
Survivour of them by me reposed & to the Intent & purpose That they the said\
William Woolven George ffranklyn & Edward Stone & the Survivour & Survivours of\
them & the Exec[uto]rs: & Adm[inistrato]rs: of the Survivour of them shall &\
will yearly dureing the Continuance of the said Terme of six yeares out of the\
Rents & proffitts of my said Messuage Lands & p[re]misses pay & satisfye the\
said Annuity or yearly Rent Charge of Tenn pounds unto my said Daughter (if she\
soe long live) at such dayes & in such Manner as the same is herein before\
menconed or appointed to be paid And dureing the Continuance of the said Terme\
as aforesd: shall & will alsoe pay & dispose of all the rest & residue of the\
Rents & proffitts of my said Messuage Lands & p[re]misses unto & for my said\
Grandsonne John Woolven to & for his sole & only use & benefitt & in\
p[ar]ticular that Twelve pounds yearly of the same proffitts (after paymt: of\
the said Annuity) be by my said Trustees paid & applyed to & for the placeing\
out & Mainteyning of my said Grandsonne at Schoole And from & after the\
expirac[i]on or other determinac[i]on of the said Estate & Terme for six yeares\
Then my Will is And I doe hereby give & Devise all & every my said Messuage\
Closes Lands Tenemts: Meadow pasture Ley ground hereditamts: & p[re]misses with\
their & every of their appurten[an]ces unto the said John Woolven my said\
Grandsonne & to the heires of his body Lawfully begotten & to be begotten & for\
Default of such Issue then I doe further

Give & Devise All my said Messuage Lands Tenemts: & p[re]misses aforemenconed\
with their & every of their appurten[an]ces unto them the said William Woolven\
George ffranklyn & Edward Stone their Exec[uto]rs: Adm[inistrato]rs: & Assignes\
for & dureing the Terme of seaven yeares to be accompted from the time of the\
Decease of my said Grandsonne soe Dyeing without Issue as aforesd: fully to be\
Compleat & ended Under & Subject neverthelesse to the Trust Condic[i]ons &\
Limitac[i]ons herein after menconed concerning the same Terme & Estate for\
seaven yeares And from & after the end & expirac[i]on or other determinac[i]on\
of the said Estate & Terme for seaven yeares Then my Will is And I doe hereby\
give & Devise All my said Messuage Closes Lands Tenemts: Meadowes pasture Ley\
ground hereditamts: & p[re]misses with their & every of their appurten[an]ces\
unto William Slater als[o] Cossendon sonne of my late Sister Anne & to the\
heires & Assignes of the said William Slater als[o] Cossendon for ever = And as\
to the said Estate & Terme for seaven yeares soe herein before by me Limited &\
Devised unto them the said William Woolven George ffranklyn & Edward Stone their\
Exec[uto]rs: Adm[inistrato]rs: & Assignes It is the true Intent & meaneing of\
this my Will & I doe hereby Declare that the said Terme & Estate for seaven\
yeares is Upon speciall Trust & Confidence & to the Intent & purpose that they\
the said William Woolven George ffranklyn & Edward Stone & the Survivour &\
Survivours of them & the Exec[uto]rs: & Adm[inistrato]rs: of the Survivour of\
them shall & will as soone as Conveniently may be by with & out of the Rents\
Issues & proffitts of all the said Messuage Lands Tenemts: & p[re]misses herein\
before menconed Levy raise & pay the severall sumes of Money herein following\
(that is to say) the sume of fforty pounds of good & Lawfull money of Great\
Brittaine unto & for Ann Bowden Daughter of my late Brother William Bowden\
deceased & the sume of Twenty pounds a peice of like Money unto & for all the\
Children of my late Sister Elizabeth Winch deceased late wife of Thomas Winch\
And upon this further Trust And my Will & Intent is that as soone as the said\
Legacyes or sumes of fforty pounds for the said Ann Bowden my said late Brothers\
Daughter & of Twenty pounds a peice for my said Sister Winches Children shall be\
Levyed & raised Or in Case the said William Slater als[o] Cossendon his heires\
or Assignes shall within two yeares next after my said Messuage Lands &\
p[re]misses shall come to him or them by vertue of this my Will well & truely\
pay & discharge the same That then they the said William Woolven George\
ffranklyn & Edward Stone their Exec[uto]rs: Adm[inistrato]rs: & Assignes shall &\
will at the reasonable Request & p[ro]per Costs & Charges of him the said\
William Slater als[o] Cossendon his heires or Assignes surrender & yeild up all\
theire said Terme & Estate for seaven yeares & pay & dispose of all Moneys by\
them to be raised or received by & out of the proffitts of the said p[re]misses\
in the last menconed Terme (& not disposed of according to this my Will) unto\
him the said William Slater als[o] Cossendon his heires & Assignes Provided\
always & I doe hereby further Will & appoint that it shall & may be Lawfull to &\
for them the said William Woolven George

ffranklyn & Edward Stone their Exec[uto]rs: Adm[inistrato]rs: & Assignes from\
time to time to Deduct & take to themselves all their reasonable Costs & Charges\
to satisfye & reimburse themselves out of my said Estate All Taxes & other\
expences as they shall necessarily Disburse & expend or be put unto in or about\
the Managemt: of the p[re]misses or the Execuc[i]on or Defence of any of the\
Trusts aforesd: And that None of them shall be accomptable for any more of the\
proffitts of the said Messuage Lands & p[re]misses than what shall come to\
theire hands severally & respectively & that none of them shall be Chargeable or\
Accountable for any [crossed out word] Deed or Receipt of the other of them (any\
thing in this my Will Conteyned to the Contrary thereof in any wise\
notwithstanding Item I give & Devise unto the said Anne my wife all & every my\
Leasehold Lands & Tenemts: whatsoever which I hold by force & vertue of any\
Lease or Leases thereof made by the right honourable Charles Earle of Carnarvan\
scituate lyeing & being in the p[ar]ish of Ilmer aforesd: in the said County of\
Bucks To have & to hold unto her the said Anne my wife for & dureing soe many\
yeares of the Terme or Termes of yeares that I have thereof & therein as\
aforesd: as shall runne & be Elapsed dureing the Naturall life of her the said\
Anne my wife yet alwayes under & subject to the Rents & Coven[an]ts: Reserved &\
Conteyned in such Lease on my part & behalfe to be done & p[er]formed And from &\
after the Decease of her the said Anne my wife (In Case the said Lease or Leases\
be not then expired) then my Will is & I doe hereby give & Devise All my\
Leasehold p[re]misses aforesd: unto the said John Woolven my said Grandson To\
have & to hold to him his Exec[uto]rs: Adm[inistrato]rs: & Assignes for &\
dureing soe many yeares of my Terme or Termes of yeares aforesd: then to come &\
unexpired as shall runne & Elapse dureing the life & lives of the said John\
Woolven & of the Issue of his body Lawfully to be begotten if any be Yet with &\
under such Rents & Coven[an]ts: as aforesd: And in Case the said John Woolven\
shall happen to dye & Issue of his body to faile & not be before the\
expirac[i]on of the Lease or Leases to me made as aforesd: Then my Will is & I\
doe hereby give & Devise all my said Leasehold Lands p[re]misses & all the rest\
& residue of my Terme & Termes of yeares as shall be thereof or therein then to\
come & unexpired unto him the said William Slater als[o] Cossendon his\
Exec[uto]rs: Adm[inistrato]rs: & Assignes yet likewise under & with the same\
Rents & Coven[an]ts: aforemenconed ITEM I doe make ordaine & appoint her the\
said Anne my wife sole Executrix of this my last Will & Testamt: And doe give\
her all my Goods Cattell Chattells & p[er]sonall Estate whatsoever\
(unbequeathed) she paying my debts & ffunerall expences & alsoe paying unto\
William Bowden sonne of my said late Brother William the sume of one shilling &\
alsoe to Thomas Bowry sonne of my late Sister Katherine the like sume of one\
shilling IN WITNESSE whereof I the said John Bowden the Testator unto this my\
last Will & Testamt: Revokeing all former Wills by me made have to this my last\
Conteyned & expressed in two sheetes of paper & halfe of a Third sheete to each

of the said sheetes sett my hand & seale the Eighteenth day of September in the\
Tenth yeare of the Reigne of o[u]r Soveraigne Lady Anne by the grace of God of\
Great Brittaine ffrance & Ireland Queene Defendor of the ffaith &c Annoq[ue]\
D[omi]ni 1711. John Bowden Signed sealed published & Declared by the above sd:\
Testator in the p[re]sence of us the witnesses whose names are hereunder written\
& who hereunto have subscribed in the p[re]sence of the Testator (vizt:) Thomas\
Young George Chapman Joseph Monday. ITEM my further Will is And I doe hereby\
give & Devise unto my Maid Mary Bastian who now lives with me the sume of five\
pounds yearly for the Terme of foure yeares for her yearly wages to live with my\
said Executrix Anne my wife at & dureing to be paid by my said Executrix yearly\
dureing her life And in Case my said Executrix should not soe long live then for\
the Terme which is to come & unexpired After her decease to live in the my said\
Messuage or Tenemt: at her uses & the said sume to be Levyed & raised out of my\
said Lands & p[re]misses And in Case my said Executrix should not be willing she\
should live with her or my Exec[uto]rs: or Assignes in this my said Messuage or\
Tenemt: then my Will is & I doe hereby give & Devise unto the said Mary Bastian\
the sume of Twenty pounds to be paid by my Executrix at the end of the said\
Terme of foure yeares Or in Case my said Executrix should not soe long live then\
the said sume of Twenty pounds to be Levyed & raised out of my said Lands &\
p[re]misses IN WITNESSE whereof I have hereunto sett my hand & seale the same\
day & yeare above written. John Bowden Signed sealed published & Declared by the\
above sd: Testator in the p[re]sence of us the Witnesses whose names are\
hereunder written & who hereunto have subscribed in the p[re]sence of the\
Testator (vizt:) the marke of Thomas Greenwood the marke of Joyce Gowin Joseph\
Monday. /:

By the tenor of these presents, We, Thomas Ayloffe, Doctor of Laws, Commissary,\
etc., and Official lawfully constituted in and throughout the whole Archdeaconry\
of Bucks, make known to all men that on the seventeenth day of the month of\
December in the year of our Lord One Thousand Seven Hundred and Eleven, before\
us [and others] lawfully proceeding, the above-written Will of John Bowden, late\
of Ilmer in the Archdeaconry of Bucks, deceased, was proved, and by him approved\
and registered, and duly and lawfully pronounced and declared for the force and\
validity of the same;

And the burden of the execution of the said Will, as well as the Administration,\
etc., of [all] and singular the goods, rights, credits, and [chattels] of the\
said deceased, pertaining to him at the [time] of his death and remaining within\
the aforesaid Archdeaconry, and in any way concerning this [testament], was\
committed to Anne Bowden, Relict (widow) of the said deceased and the Executrix\
named in the same Will, she, the said Anne Bowden, having taken the oath\
according to the Act of Parliament published and provided in that behalf (by\
virtue of a Commission made in that behalf), saving the right of whomsoever.

In testimony whereof we have caused the seal which we use [in this behalf] to be\
affixed to these presents. Given on the day, month, and year [aforesaid].

J. Lodington, Surrogate.
