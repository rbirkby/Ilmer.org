This is the last Will and Testament\
of me Moreton John Edward Frewen of 31 Sussex Square Brighton\
Revoking all former Wills and Codicils by me made Imprimis I give and devise\
my copyhold farm and lands situate in the Parish of Great Bentley in the County\
of Essex together with the great tithes thereof to the use of my relation John Jenkin\
now or late of Stouting in Kent and his heirs and assigns for ever because he was\
a good son to his Father and as for and concerning all such of my lands and\
hereditaments situate in the Parish of Beckley in the County of Sussex as are of\
copyhold tenure I confirm my Fathers Will in respect thereof and subject thereto I\
devise the same To the use of Charles Francis Lord of Hampstead Surgeon and his\
heirs and assigns for ever And as for and concerning my manor or reputed manor\
of Ilmer or Illmire and all my farms lands tenements tithes and hereditaments\
situate in the Parishes of Ilmer or Illmire Saunderton and Kingsey or elsewhere in\
the County of Bucks I give and devise the same To the use of my dear Wife Sarah\
Frewen for and during the term of her natural life without impeachment of waste\
voluntary or permissive of any sort or kind whatever with power to my said Wife\
to make leases or agreements for leases at rack rent of the same or any part or\
parts thereof for any term or terms not exceeding fourteen years and a day and to\
commence or to be computed either from the day of the execution thereof respectively or\
some day prior to or within six calendar months after the same And from and\
after the decease of my said Wife I give and devise my said manor or reputed\
manor and all my farms lands tenements tithes and hereditaments in the County of\
Bucks To the use of Frances Annabella Moreton Craigie eldest daughter of my late\
uncle the Reverend William Moreton Moreton of Westerham in Kent for and during\
the term of her natural life for her own exclusive use and benefit apart and distinct\
from her present or any future husband and not to be subject to his debts controul or\
engagements without impeachment of waste as aforesaid and with like power to\
her to make leases or agreements for leases And from and after her decease I give\
and devise the same manor or reputed manor farms lands and hereditaments\
in the County of Bucks To the use of her sister Elizabeth Moreton for and during the\
term of her natural life for her own exclusive use and benefit apart and distinct

from any husband she may marry and not to be subject to his debts controul\
or engagements without impeachment of waste as aforesaid and with like\
power to make leases or agreements for leases And from and after the decease\
of the survivor of the said sisters To the use of the first and other son and sons of\
the said Frances Annabella Moreton Craigie in tail general severally successively\
and in remainder one after another according to priority of birth and in default\
of such issue To the use of her first and other daughter and daughters in tail\
general severally successively and in remainder one after another according to\
priority of birth and in default of such issue To the use of the first and other son\
and sons of the said Elizabeth Moreton in tail general severally successively\
and in remainder one after another according to priority of birth And in default\
of such issue To the use of the first and other daughter and daughters of the said\
Elizabeth Moreton in tail general severally successively and in remainder\
according to priority of birth And in default of all such issue To the use of my\
relation Thomas Frewen of Brickwall House in the County of Sussex Esqr for and\
during the term of his natural life without impeachment of any manner of waste\
as aforesaid and with like power to make leases or agreements for leases as afore-\
said And from and after his decease To the use of his daughter Eleanor Frewen\
her heirs and assigns for ever for her separate use and benefit apart and distinct\
from any husband she may marry and not to be subject to his debts controul or\
engagements And as for and concerning my farm lands and hereditaments in the\
Parish of Remenham or elsewhere in the County of Berks I give and devise the\
same To the use of my dear Wife the said Sarah Frewen for and during the term\
of her natural life without impeachment of waste as aforesaid and with like\
power to make leases or agreements for leases as aforesaid And from and after her\
decease To the use of my relation Charles Hay Frewen late of Coghurst in the County\
of Sussex Esquire and his heirs and assigns for ever And as for and concerning my\
farm lands and hereditaments situate in the Parish of Icklesham in the County\
of Sussex I give and devise the same To the use of John Frewen eldest son of the\
aforesaid Thomas Frewen his heirs and assigns for ever but subject to and\
charged with the annuity or rent charge hereinafter mentioned and charged\
upon the same And as for and concerning my house lands and hereditaments\
called Farthings situate in the Parish of Northiam in the County of Sussex And\
also all such of my lands and hereditaments situate in the Parish of Beckley\
aforesaid as are of freehold tenure except the undivided moiety of lands and\
hereditaments late the property of the late Sarah Chittenden of Hawkhurst in

the County of Kent deceased and also my farm called Strawberry Hole in\
Northiam aforesaid and all other my lands ^cottages^ gardens and hereditaments situate\
in Northiam aforesaid I give and devise the same except the undivided moiety\
hereinbefore excepted To the use of my said Wife Sarah Frewen her heirs and assigns\
for ever And I give and bequeath to Mary Ann Plumley daughter of John Plumley\
late of Northiam aforesaid the annuity or yearly sum of twenty pounds for and\
during the term of her natural life free and clear from legacy duty by even and\
equal half yearly payments on the nineteenth day of January and the nineteenth\
day of July in every year the first of such payments to be made on such one of the\
said days as shall as shall happen next after my decease and I charge this same\
annuity upon my farm lands and hereditaments situate at Icklesham aforesaid\
And as concerning the personal estate with which it has pleased the Almighty to bless\
me I confirm the right of my said Wife if she survives me to take as survivor such\
part and parts thereof as stand in her name jointly with my own and as to the\
residue of my personal estate standing in my own name only I give and bequeath\
the same subject to the payment of my debts funeral expences and the legacies herein-\
after mentioned to my said dear Wife my will and pleasure being that the said\
bequest and the devises to her hereinbefore contained shall be in lieu bar and satis-\
faction of all her right to dower or thirds by common law statute or otherwise and\
also of all claims ^which^ she may have under my Fathers Will And I give and\
bequeath to my Wifes Housekeeper Jane Jarrett Widow the annuity or yearly sum

of twenty six pounds for and during the term of her natural life free and clear\
from legacy duty And I give and bequeath unto my cousin the said Charles\
Hay Frewen the sum of Five Hundred pounds And I nominate constitute and\
appoint the said Charles Hay Frewen and my said Wife Executor and Executrix\
of this my Will Witness my hand this sixteenth day of May in the year of our Lord\
1867 – Moreton J. E. Frewen – Signed published and declared by the within\
named Moreton John Edward Frewen as and for his last Will and Testament in\
the presence of us who in his presence and that of each other have hereunto set our\
names as witnesses hereto – Arthur Walton Cornwall Clerk Midhurst,\
Edward Tufnell Clerk, Easebourne, Sussex –

THIS is a Codicil to be added to and taken as part of the last Will and\
Testament of me Moreton John Edward Frewen I hereby constitute and\
appoint the Revd Henry Jenkins of Stanway near Colchester Essex Brother to my dear\
deceased Wife to be my joint Executor with Charles Hay Frewen And I give and\
bequeath to him the sum of Ten thousand pounds ^New^ Bradford Stock And I give\
and bequeath to my House keeper Jane Jarrett Widow the annuity or yearly sum\
of Seventy pounds in lieu of and instead of the annuity of Twenty six pounds which\
I hereby revoke for and during the term of her natural life free and clear from\
legacy duty if she continues my House keeper till the time of my decease Witness\
my hand this eighteenth day of February in the year of our Lord 1869 – Moreton\
J. E. Frewen – Signed published and declared by the above named Moreton John\
Edward Frewen as and for a Codicil to his last Will and Testament in the presence of\
us who in his presence and that of each other have hereunto set our names as\
witnesses hereto the words (New Bradford Stock) being first inserted – Samuel\
Barker M.D 14 Eaton Place Brighton – Frances Mary Barker 14 Eaton\
Place Brighton.

Proved at London with a Codicil 19th June 1871 by the oath of Charles Hay Frewen\
Esqre the surviving Executor named in the Will and the Revd Henry Jenkins Clerk\
(the Brother of Testator's late Wife) the Executor named in the said Codicil to whom\
Admon was granted.
