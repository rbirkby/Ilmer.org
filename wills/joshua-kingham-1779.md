Kingham Joshua 44

In the name of God Amen I Joshua Kingham the\
elder of Ilmer in the County of Bucks Husbandman\
being sick and weak in body but of perfect and sound\
mind memory and understanding and being mindful\
of my mortality do hereby make ordain publish and\
declare this my last will and testament in writing\
as followeth revoking hereby and setting aside all\
former and other wills by me made either in\
writing or by word <sup>of</sup> mouth And First and principally\
I commend my soul into the hands of Almighty God\
who gave it me in hopes through the merrits of my\
redeemer Jesus Christ to have pardon for all my\
sins and to have everlasting life and my body I committ to\
the earth to be decently interred at the discretion of my\
executor herein after named and as to my temporal estate which

---

Which it hath pleased the Almighty to enrich me with\
I give will and dispose of the same as followeth And First\
I give will and bequeath unto my beloved daughter\
Mary Kingham the sum of sixty pounds of lawful money\
of Great <sup>Britain</sup> to be paid to my said daughter Mary her\
executors or assigns in one year after my decease out\
of my personal estate by my executor herein after named\
Also I give will and bequeath unto my beloved daughter\
Sarah Kingham the sum of sixty pounds of the like\
lawful money of Great Britain to be paid unto my\
said Daughter Sarah her executors or assigns in two\
years after my decease out of my personal estate by\
my executor herein after named Also I give will and\
bequeath unto my beloved daughter Elizabeth Kingham\
the sum of sixty pounds of the like good and lawful money\
of Great Britain to be paid to my said Daughter\
Elizabeth her executors or assigns in three years after\
my decease out of my personal estate by my executor\
herein after named Also I give will and bequeath\
unto my beloved daughter Hannah Kingham the sum\
of forty pounds of the like lawful money of Great Britain\
to be paid to my said daughter Hannah her executors or\
assigns out of my personal estate in four years after\
my decease by my executor herein after named Also I give\
will and bequeath unto my beloved daughter Ann West\
the sum of Forty pounds of the like lawful money of\
Great Britain to be paid to my said daughter Ann her\
executors or assigns out of my personal estate by my executor

---

herein after named in five years after my decease\
Also I Give will and bequeath unto my beloved\
daughter Jane Kingham the sum of Fifty pounds\
of the like lawful money of Great Britain to be\
paid to my said daughter Jane out of my personal\
estate in six years after my decease by my executor\
herein after named Provided always and upon\
this condition Nevertheless that if my said Daughter\
Jane Kingham shall fortune to decease before she arrives\
to the age of twenty one years that then my will and meaning\
is And I do hereby give will and bequeath the said sum\
of fifty pounds bequeathed into her as aforesaid in manner\
following (that is to say) ten pounds thereof unto my\
daughter Hannah Kingham aforesaid and ten pounds\
more thereof to my said Daughter Ann West to be paid\
to my said two daughters Hannah and Ann respectively\
in six years after my decease out of my personal estate\
as aforesaid and the remaining sum of thirty pounds\
out of the aforesaid legacy of Fifty pounds shall go\
be and remain in the hands of my executor herein\
after named any thing in this my last will contained\
to the contrary thereof in anywise notwithstanding Also\
my will and meaning is that my said daughter\
Jane Kingham shall have free liberty and priviledge\
to dwell and inhabit with my executor herein after named\
and to partake of his common Meat drink washing\
lodging and apparel fitting for a person of such a degree\
until she arrives to the age of twenty one years if she\
shall so long live And lastly I give will and bequeath\
unto my beloved son John Kingham (after paying my

---

Just debts and funeral expenses) all and singular\
and every my money apparel stock of corn Grain hay\
Implements of husbandry horses cows sheep hogs goods\
chattels leases credits and all other my person estate\
whatsoever herein before unbequeathed to go be and\
remain unto my said son John Kingham his executors\
administrators or assigns to his and their own proper use\
behoof and disposal And I do hereby charge all and\
every my said personal estate with the payment of\
the several legacies and payments aforesaid according\
to the true Intent and meaning hereof And I do hereby\
nominate ordain constitute and appoint my said son\
John Kingham to be <sup>my</sup> sole executor to this my last will\
and testament contained in two sheets of paper unto the\
first of which I have Set my hand and to the second and\
last my hand and seal this twenty second day of\
February in the year of our Lord One thousand seven\
hundred and seventy nine Joshua Kingham\
Signed sealed published and declared by the testator\
to be his last will and testament in the presence of us\
who have subscribed our names as witnesses to the same\
in the presence of the testator and at his request The\
mark of Allice West of Owlswick The\
mark of Susannah Adams of Ilmer\
Henry Lacey of P Risbro

---

Proved the 6th day of July 1779\
before the reverend William Stockins Clerk Surrogate\
by the oath of John Kingham the natural and lawful son\
and sole executor to whom Adcon was committed\
he being first sworn duly to administer
